During this project, Team Car RamRod aims to learn about the various solutions for analyzing malware while protecting the entire virtualized environment from attack.

The goal of Team Car RamRod, for this project, is to generalize and document an enterprise-level malware solution that can be used to analyze and protect any virutalized business.

Since all of our team members are novices when it comes to virtualization, many of our goals (and future work) will be discovered along our journey of research and experience.

For more information, read our project proposal in Downloads.

Our project documentation will also be posted in the coming weeks.
